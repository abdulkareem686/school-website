Admissions at Al-hamdulillahi Academy

Find all the information you need to apply to our school.

Admission Process

Outline the steps involved in the admission process clearly and concisely.

  1. Step 1: Inquiry and Application Form - Obtain the admission form from the school's administrative office or download it from our website.
  2. Step 2: Submission of Application - Complete the application form and submit it along with the required documents (e.g., birth certificate, previous academic records, passport photographs).
  3. Step 3: Entrance Examination - All applicants will be required to sit for an entrance examination covering core subjects.
  4. Step 4: Interview - Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with the school's admission committee.
  5. Step 5: Admission Offer - Successful candidates will receive an admission offer letter.
  6. Step 6: Acceptance and Enrollment - Accept the offer and complete the enrollment process by paying the necessary fees and submitting any remaining documents.

Required Documents

List all the documents that need to be submitted along with the application form.

  • Completed Admission Form
  • Original Birth Certificate
  • Photocopy of Previous Academic Records/Transcripts
  • Two Recent Passport Photographs
  • Letter of Recommendation (if applicable)
  • Payment Receipt for Application Fee

Admission Criteria

Outline the criteria used for evaluating applicants.

  • Performance in the Entrance Examination
  • Performance in the Interview
  • Review of Previous Academic Records
  • Age Appropriateness for the Grade Level
